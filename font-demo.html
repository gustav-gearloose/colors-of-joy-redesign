Font Selector Demo – Choose your perfect typography combination

Welcome to Colors of Joy

This is a demonstration of the global font selector. You can choose different fonts for headers and body text, and the changes will apply across all pages on the site. Your preferences are saved in your browser's localStorage.

Header Font Preview

The header font is applied to all h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, and h6 elements. Try selecting different decorative fonts like Rastanty Cortex, Modern Love, or calligraphic styles to see how they transform the headers.

Body Font Preview

The body font is applied to all paragraph text and general content. Choose readable serif fonts like Georgia or Garamond for classic elegance, or clean sans-serif fonts like Helvetica for a modern look.

Font Categories

Handwriting: Casual, flowing scripts perfect for a personal touch. Examples include Rastanty Cortex, Lucida Handwriting, and Segoe Print.

Calligraphy: Elegant, formal scripts ideal for sophisticated headers. Try Apple Chancery, Modern Love, or Pristina.

Serif: Traditional fonts with decorative strokes, excellent for both headers and body text. Georgia, Garamond, and Baskerville offer timeless elegance.

Sans-serif: Clean, modern fonts without decorative strokes. Helvetica, Avenir, and Futura provide contemporary style.

How It Works

The font selector uses CSS custom properties (CSS variables) to apply fonts globally. When you select a font, it updates the --font-header and --font-body variables, which are referenced throughout the site's stylesheets. Your selections are saved to localStorage, so they persist across page refreshes and navigation.
